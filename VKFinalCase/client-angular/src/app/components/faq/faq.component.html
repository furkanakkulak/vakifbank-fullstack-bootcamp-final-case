Frequently asked questions

What is VKStore?

VKStore is a platform developed to manage product orders and delivery processes between you, the retailers, and us, the manufacturers. You can easily handle order creation, stock tracking, payment methods, and much more.

How Can I Use VKStore?

To start using VKStore, firstly, we establish agreements by communicating with you and determining your profit margins. Then, we create an account for you, catering to your retail needs. You can browse products, place orders, and track deliveries.

Which Payment Methods Do We Accept?

We accept various payment methods on VKStore, including credit cards, EFT, and wire transfers. During the ordering process, you can choose your preferred payment method.

When Will My Orders Be Delivered?

The delivery time for your orders varies based on product availability and the delivery address. You can track the status of your orders through your VKStore account and access up-to-date information.

How Can I Check Product Stock Availability?

Through the VKStore application, you can view detailed stock information for products. You can keep track of the current quantities available and monitor stock availability in real-time.

How Can I Secure My VKStore Account?

To keep your VKStore account secure, ensure that you access your account only from trusted devices and secure internet connections. Additionally, remember to log out securely after using your session.

What Support Services Do We Provide?

Our VKStore customer support team is here to assist you with any issues related to the application. Whether it's order tracking, payment problems, technical issues, or other concerns, feel free to get in touch with us for support.

How Can I Discover New Products on VKStore?

We constantly update VKStore with new products. To discover these products, log in to your VKStore account, visit the "New Products" section on the homepage, or select "Newly Added Products" from the categories tab. You can explore detailed information and features of new products by visiting their respective product pages.
